Meet the Meetups

The tech industry in the Fargo/Moorhead area is exploding. In the best way possible. Tech Tailgate wants to give you the opportunity to check out some of the many different meetup groups in the area. Each meetup will be "tailgating" in the Fargo Brewing Company parking lot, strutting their stuff and showing you exaclty what they're about. We have meetups focused the web, mobile, education, content strategy, and just plain geeks and nerds!
