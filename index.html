A meeup of the meetups. A celebration bringing together the entire tech community and each group that dedicates its time and energy towards pushing the indstury forward.

Bringing the tech industry together, one tailgate at a time.

The technology industry is constantly changing and evolving. It's building and growing cities, it's changing lives and bringing people together. We've noticed that with every day, there are more and more tech meetup groups, each with a different purpose, a different mission. It's the goal of tech tailgate to bring together people from every group, to celebrate the tech community in all its corners.

The first Tech Tailgate was born in the booming town of Fargo, ND. Held in a local brewery parking lot, tech meetup groups came tiny cars, jacked-up trucks, and some crossover SUVs. They lined the lot, opened their tailgates, and strut their stuff. Beer was drank, pizza was ate, and the fun lasted far later into the night than it should have on a Thursday evening.

Tech Tailgate has since grown from that first parking lot party to be a highly-antipated, annual celebration of the tech industry, spreading to cities across the US. Find out if your city's on the list!
